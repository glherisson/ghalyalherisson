Meet Melissa Maximilien!
Melissa Maximilien was born and raised in Haiti then moved to Miami, Florida to begin her college years. For the past 15 years she has been working as a consultant with Interval International, a time-share company. But while working in Corporate America she grew a passion for event planning. It began as a hobby, Melissa would plan her friends and family parties but in 2013 she decided to start a business. She would call this business, "Meli Max Event Services". Melissa decided to quit her job from Corporate America and begin working part-time from her home as an event planner while also being a full-time mother to her two children. Meli Max Event Services mission is to provide you with a once in a lifetime experience.
